Rev. Brer Kat Meow Tse-Tongue

Rev. Brer Kat, Director of Meowtaphysics and Predacious Studies, is the Abyssinian who rules Rev. Jeff. He leads the DOFC Taskforce and produced this chart. He's not at all interested in hearing any of your shit..


Zoe the WonderBaby

Zoe is now more than three years old. Under Rev. Ralph's tutelage, she has already mastered Sanskrit and Russian, and has written a paper criticizing Newtonian physics which was published in Duke University's Subtext magazine. Zoe currently holds a fellowship at Oberlin in Ohio.

Ultra-High Priest Bobby Gelner [incarcerated]
UHP Gelner was responsible for SuperChurch® in the sense that he was the first mortal contacted by the Benign All. The UHP is presently unemployed and not receiving his paltry GI benefits due to an unfortunate assault on Sheriff who was "way too frisky." UHP Gelner is up for work release soon, and he would welcome any employment opportunity. His back is strong and he can "outwork 10 of them illegals." He lives in Egypt, TX.


Rev. Jeff

Reverend Jeff, Director of Metaphysics, is doing something vaguely related to law in the Twin Cities. He reports being very happy to be out of Iowa, and is apparantly not yet aware of the winters yet to come.


Rev. GREP

Reverend GREP, Director of Information, wrote his own bio.


Rev. Davo [suspended]

Reverend Davo, Director of Charisma, having conquered most of Europe and Northern Africa, is presently living in pre-nuptial bliss in some incestual town in North Carolina. He has been very tightlipped concerning future goals, but has made vague references to both physics and stock car racing.. The High Council has placed Rev. Davo on indefinite suspension for techno-weeniness, and general accusations of spinelessness (though we must admit, it's awfully charismatic spinelessness).


Shaanti the SuperNurse

SuperNurse Shaanti is a nurse practitioner in Austin. She specializes in labor and delivery, which is of precious little use to those of us who just want some needless prescriptions for liquid valium.  Rev. Shaanti is wholly against any and all of our actions, and accepted an appointment to the High Council merely to keep us silly boys out of trouble.


Rev. Mollie

Rev. Mollie, SuperChurch Hair Czar, is a heartless advertising exec in NYC. She spends her 26 hour days figuring how close the carrot can be dangled in front of the nose before the sucker catches on and changes the channel. But she's got fabulous hair.


Rev. Biji

Reverend Bijou, shown here with Rev. Ralph, is a fluffy bichon frise currently residing in Boca Raton, FL. He spends his days consulting with Rev. Ralph and worrying that the cats aren't being disciplined nearly enough. Rev. Biji is still quite angry at Rev. Jeff for kidnapping Rev. Ralph and taking him to Texas.


Rev. Ralph

Reverend Ralph is what one might call a bone, if one were incredibly callous. He prefers to be referred to formally, or as an Ossified American. Rev. Ralph holds over 129 Master's degrees, in subjects ranging from diction to relationships to microwaving. His recently published paper, researched in Lake Louise, Alberta, Canada, proved his thesis that all the clams there are happy. Along with Rev. Biji, Rev. Ralph is currently not speaking to Rev. Jeff.
